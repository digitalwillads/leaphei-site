How a Leap HEI works.

A home equity investment works in three steps, and the repayment only comes due when you sell the home, refinance it, or reach the end of the term you chose at closing.

Step One

Receive cash

You receive a lump sum of up to $300,000, deposited directly to your account after closing.

Step Two

No monthly obligations

Because the investment is not a loan, there are no monthly payments and no interest rate accruing in the background, so your household budget continues exactly as it did the day before closing.

Step Three

Repay when you're ready

When you sell your home, refinance, or reach the end of the term, you repay the original investment plus a share of your home's appreciation.
