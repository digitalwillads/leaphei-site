Privacy Policy

A plain-English statement of what data Leap collects, why, and what we do with it. The official, attorney-reviewed version controls. This page summarizes it.

Last updated: April 2026. Placeholder content — final policy will be provided by counsel before launch.

What we collect

To pre-qualify you, we collect the state your home is in, an estimate of your equity, and a credit-range bucket. We do not pull your credit at pre-qualification.

If you continue to a full application, we collect a soft credit profile, property records, contact details, and the documents you upload to support the file.

Why we collect it

To make underwriting decisions, to verify your identity under federal regulations, and to administer your investment if you accept one. We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing.

Your rights

You may request a copy of your data, correct it, or ask us to delete it at any time, subject to record-retention requirements imposed by federal and state regulators on financial-service providers.
