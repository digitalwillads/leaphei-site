About Leap

A team that has spent careers in the equity of homes.

Leap Analytics, Inc. was founded in 2022 to bring a true equity-investment product to the U.S. homeowner market. Our team brings decades of combined experience across mortgage origination, real-estate investment, regulatory compliance, and consumer fintech.

Founder, CEO & CIO

Ashley Bete

A twenty-year veteran of financial technology, with earlier leadership roles spanning Credit Suisse, Cantor Fitzgerald, and Nasdaq OMX. Founded Leap in 2022 to build a true equity-investment product for the U.S. homeowner, on the conviction that the category had been mispositioned by every existing operator.

Chief Technology Officer

Believes technology earns its keep when it improves the lives of the people who have historically been underserved by it, and has built Leap's platform around that idea.

Chief Operating Officer

Partners

The institutions behind every investment.

Capital partner

Institutional investors

Leap is funded by a diversified group of institutional investors including pension funds, endowments, and family offices — not a single venture-style capital pool. This structure means our investments are sized for long-term hold, not for short-term portfolio recycling.

Appraisal network

Third-party appraisers

Every Leap investment is appraised by a licensed, third-party appraiser drawn from a national network. Appraisers are paid a flat fee regardless of outcome — their compensation is not tied to whether the investment closes.

Compliance

Federally chartered counsel

All Leap contracts are reviewed by federally chartered consumer-finance counsel and benchmarked against TILA, RESPA, and state-level equity-investment statutes — including the recent California, Colorado, and Connecticut rule packages.
