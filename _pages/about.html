Why work with Leap?

Leap’s mission is to help homeowners improve their financial wellness by unlocking the equity in their homes and providing a range of financial services that create wealth mobility.

Leap combines advanced financial technology with institutional real estate expertise in order to:

  • Improve homeowners’ financial literacy
  • Allow homeowners to tap into their home’s equity through Home Equity Agreements
  • Provide a full suite of products and services that support financial wellness
  • Increase homeowners’ financial power through smart, long-term planning

Leap, headquartered in Los Angeles, California, is a real estate investment company providing education and purpose-built Home Equity Agreements (HEAs). At Leap, we believe in teamwork and having fun when we’re not grinding on your behalf.
