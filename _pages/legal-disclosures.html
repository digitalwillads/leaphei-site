A Leap HEI is not a loan

A home equity investment is structured as an equity investment, not a debt instrument. It does not appear on your credit report, but it does encumber the title of your home for the duration of the investment.

Repayment can exceed the original investment

If your home appreciates significantly during the investment term, your repayment will exceed the original investment amount. The appreciation share is set at closing and never changes during the term.

Repayment can be less than the original investment

If your home declines in value, your repayment will be reduced. In severe declines, the repayment can fall below the original investment.

Tax treatment

Tax treatment of an HEI depends on your jurisdiction and individual circumstances. Consult a tax professional before relying on tax assumptions in your decision.
