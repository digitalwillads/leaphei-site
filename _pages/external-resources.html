Consumer Lending Resources

consumerfinance.gov — The Consumer Financial Protection Bureau website provides a wealth of information focused on protecting consumers everywhere.

mymoney.gov — Dedicated to teaching all Americans the basics about financial education and sponsored by the U.S. Financial Literacy and Education Commission. Covers topics like buying a home, balancing a checkbook, and investing in a 401(k) plan.

ftc.gov — The Federal Trade Commission offers information and protection to consumers to help them avoid fraud and make wise money decisions.
