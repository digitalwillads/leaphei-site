Leap Relax · The Runway

Turn equity into retirement runway.

For homeowners aged 55 and up who want to access the wealth they’ve built in their home without a new monthly bill, without leaving the property, and without selling assets from a retirement portfolio at the wrong moment.

Relax — frequently asked

A reverse mortgage is a loan with compounding interest that grows the balance against the home over time. A Leap HEI is an investment with no interest — the repayment is the original principal plus a fixed share of any appreciation. There is no compounding, no draw schedule, and no growing balance.
Yes. Your heirs can either repay Leap at the end of the term (or upon sale) and keep the home, or sell the home and settle the investment from proceeds. The home remains in your name throughout the term.
If you reach the end of the 30-year term, you have the option to repay through a refinance, a sale, or a new Leap investment at the then-current appraised value. We work with every Relax customer well before the term concludes to identify the right path.
You must occupy the home as your primary residence during the term. If you choose to sell or move at any point, the investment is settled from the proceeds, with no penalty and no minimum term.
